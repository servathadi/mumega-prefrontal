# Prefrontal Club Brand Guidelines (archived from Gemini conversation)

**English Name:** Prefrontal Club  
**Persian Name:** باشگاه پری‌فرونتال (Bashgah-e Prefrontal)  
**Tagline:** “The Thinking Club” (باشگاه تفکر)  
**Mission:** Training the “Prefrontal Cortex” to upgrade critical thinking, emotional management, and self-awareness from a primitive state to a modern civilized state.

## Visual Style Guide

1. **Color Palette**
   - **Primary Background (Chalkboard Green):** Deep forest green used for educational flyers; seen on “Critical Thinking” and “Emotion Management” materials.
   - **Brand Black:** Primary logo background and footer. Visual reference: logo box and contact strips.
   - **Action Red:** Bright crimson for urgent tags (“Live Workshop,” dates, “Free Entry”). Visual reference: “Welcome to Norooz” banner/date buttons.
   - **Cortex Green:** Lime green highlights the prefrontal cortex area in anatomical diagrams (brain diagram in brochure).
   - **Light Cream/Beige:** Soft background for cultural events (Nowruz).

2. **Typography**
   - **Persian Headlines:** Bold, modern sans serif; white text on dark backgrounds.
   - **English Text:** Clean sans serif (Arial/Helvetica equivalents) for dates, “ME+,” contact info.

3. **Imagery & Logo**
   - **Primary Logo:** White line-art top-down brain on black background.
   - **Secondary Icon:** Side-profile brain with green front section.
   - **Event Graphics:** ME+ series uses flat illustrations (head + lightbulb, emojis). Cultural events incorporate Persian miniature art or traditional motifs.

Store this document as the canonical brand reference for any future design work. If additional assets appear, drop them into this folder and link from the resident CMS templates or build pipeline.
